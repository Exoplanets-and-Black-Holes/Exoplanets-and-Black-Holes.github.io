About EBH

Exoplanets and Black Holes, frequently abbreviated as EBH, is a small podcast operated by one person, me Jonas, that talks about all things space. The podcast will operate with multiple series operating under different upload rates. EBH is available on Spotify, Apple Podcasts, YouTube, and more.

What is Weekly Space Recap?

Weekly Space Recap is a quick ~10 minute recap of what happened in spaceflight the week prior. New episodes are published every Sunday at 9:25am EST and cover what happened from Sunday to Sunday
